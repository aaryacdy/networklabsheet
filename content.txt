							<h2>Kathford - where learning extends beyond classroom</h2>
										<p>Kathford International College of Engineering and Management is a top-ranked engineering and management college affiliated to Tribhuvan University where teaching extends outside classroom enabling students to develop technical expertise and professional skills which leads to excellent learning experience and outstanding employability.  At Kathford, we believe that you learn better when you are encouraged to think free, are provided exceptional resources and environment to nurture your knowledge and are engaged in analytical discourse. We learn from you to help you learn better, and that’s why we focus not only in classroom learning, but also in numerous activities that sharpen your knowledge and hone your skills. At Kathford, we offer you much more than just a qualification.</p>
